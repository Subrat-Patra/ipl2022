RAJASTHAN ROYALS

ABOUT

Rajasthan Royals (RR) is a Jaipur-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise. RR is one of the original eight IPL franchises when IPL began in 2008. At present RR is owned by Manoj Badale along with other investors. Rajasthan Royals' home ground is Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur.
