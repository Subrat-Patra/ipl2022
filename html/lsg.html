LUCKNOW SUPER GIENTS

ABOUT

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) are a franchise cricket team based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2021, the team will play its home matches at the BRSABV Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow. The team is captained by KL Rahul and coached by Andy Flower
