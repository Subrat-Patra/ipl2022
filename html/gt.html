GUJURAT TITANS

ABOUT

Gujarat Titans is a franchise cricket team based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, that started playing in the Indian Premier League (IPL) in the 2022 season. Founded in 2021, Gujarat Titans' home ground is Narendra Modi Stadium in Motera. The franchise is owned by CVC Capital Partners.
